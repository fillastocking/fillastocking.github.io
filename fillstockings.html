Fill Stockings

  • Starting at the end of October, pick up empty Christmas stockings from one of our many drop-off sites. If your group would like a large number of stockings (10 or more) contact us at info@fillastocking.org or leave a message at 503-632-0577.
  • Select the age and gender of your choice; shop for the basic items listed here (PDF) (also printed on the insert inside of each stocking) plus some additional “special items.” See the suggestions (PDF) for each age group.
  • Leave all items unwrapped, remove price tags, mark the stocking tag for age and gender then fill the stocking.
  • Return the stockings to one of our drop-off sites before the first Friday of December. Stockings returned during the month of November help us get stockings ready for the agencies. This allows us time to fill the many stocking requests. We can take stockings beyond this date as we start filling stockings again at the start of the New Year.
  • You can contact us at 503-632-0577 (messages only) or email us at info@fillastocking.org with additional questions.
