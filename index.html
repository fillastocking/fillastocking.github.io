Join us for BINGO & Help us find a new workshop space!


We are losing our workshop! Although the space we currently utilize is approximately 2,500 square feet, we would downsize to as little as 1,200 square feet if a free space could be found. We would like to continue to fill stockings for many years to come and need a workshop space to do so! Any help or leads on workshop space are appreciated. Though we cannot pay rent, we are a 501(c) nonprofit organization, thus the cost of donating space to us is tax-deductible.

Thank You
