Empty Christmas stockings have been dropped off at several businesses throughout Clackamas County. Pick up a stocking, fill it and return to where you got the stocking. Early returns in October and November help us fill the requests by the agencies we help. Donations of single items we use like toothpaste, toothbrushes, shampoo, socks, and gloves especially for adults are always needed. Cash donations help us purchase these items in bulk. Checks can be mailed to: FASFAH, PO Box 1255, Oregon City, OR 97045. You can also donate via: paypal.me/FASFAH or through Pledge at https://www.pledge.to/pledge.to.organizations/93-1296743/fill-a-stocking-fill-a-heart. (Pledge sends you a receipt as soon as you donate, and we are sent a confirmation.) Pick up a Blue Bag to fill and donate your recyclable cans and bottles (drop them off at a Bottle Drop location or the Fill a Stocking, Fill a Heart workshop located at 358 Warner Milne Rd G117, Oregon City). This is a year-round fundraiser. Purchase listed items from Amazon and items will be shipped directly to Fill a Stocking Fill a Heart. Share your Fred Meyer rewards: Every time you shop, you help Fill a Stocking Fill a Heart receive a donation. You still earn your Rewards and Fuel Points. Go to www.fredmeyer.com/communityrewards to sign up; our non-profit number is 91156. If you haven’t re-registered, please sign up again. Our workshop is open for individuals and groups of 5 to help fill stockings at our location – 358 Warner Milne Rd G117, Oregon City. We can be available Monday-Saturday for two-hour time slots -10am to noon, 1pm to 3pm or 3pm to 5pm. You can email us at info@fillastocking.org or text Willy at 503-351-2628 to request a date and time to volunteer. Like to sew or knit? We have a pattern and pre-cut kits to sew our stockings and pre-cut kits for pillowcases. We also have material and some yarn at our workshop. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us. Thank you, Fill a Stocking, Fill a Heart

Donations


We can use the following:
Gloves, thick socks, toothbrushes, toothpaste, dental floss, chap stick, mild bar soap, small to medium-sized tubes of lotion, deodorant, shampoo and conditioner (16 oz or less), hairbrushes, combs (no points), face masks.

For the Homeless we can use:
Small fleece blankets, hand and foot warmers, Mylar emergency blankets, rain ponchos, small hand sanitizers, face masks, and small Dollar Tree First Aid Kits

Cash Donations


You can donate through PayPal on this website or purchase items directly through our Amazon wish list. Items will ship to our treasurer's address.


As a 501(c)3 non-profit, donations are tax deductible as allowed by the IRS. We accept and appreciate donations all through the year. Monetary donations which allow us to purchase needed items on sale and in bulk can be made through PayPal on our website or mailed to:
