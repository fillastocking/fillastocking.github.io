Help us fill over 3,500 Stockings

Help us fill over 3,500 Christmas stockings for agencies in Clackamas County during the holiday season. The stockings go to their neediest clients and are often the only gift received.

Empty stockings are available at over 35 businesses throughout the County. See our list of businesses to learn where you can pick up an empty stocking from a local business.

Especially needed are knit hats, gloves, and socks for adults. We really need stockings filled for teens and adult males. Two pairs of socks are put in each stocking so donations of adult size socks are greatly appreciated. That’s over 7000 socks needed each year.

Cash donations are always welcome as they allow us to purchase the most needed items in bulk.
