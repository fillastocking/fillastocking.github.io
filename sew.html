Sew Stockings or Donate Fabric and Yarn

Our sewing coordinator Marcelle, has a pattern FILL A STOCKING, FILL A HEART uses to ensure our stockings are the same size. She has stockings cut out and ready to sew or you can use your own Christmas fabric. Marcelle will provide a pattern and go over instructions with you.

We accept fabric donations year-round for making our Christmas stockings and pillowcases. Neutral Christmas prints can be used for all ages and genders, but any and all Christmas material is greatly appreciated. We can also use solid colors in greens, reds, blues, and gold. Lightweight denim and masculine prints can also be used to make male stockings. Non-Christmas fun kidâ€™s prints, especially for babies and younger ages, can also be used for stockings.

Floral and other prints not suitable for Christmas stockings are great for making pillowcases. Fabric for children and male pillowcases is also needed.

Knit or crochet? We love handmade hats and scarves. Donations of knitted items and yarn accepted all year, too.
