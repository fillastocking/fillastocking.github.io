Donate items all year long!

Your school, business, church or other community organization can help by collecting needed items.

  • Set dates for your collection event - it can occur anytime during the year!
  • Decorate a box in which the items will be placed – we can send you a sign and logo to place on it.
  • Contact us for pickup of items or drop off locations.

Schools may want to have a “contest” between classrooms to collect the most items. If so, emphasize that all items must be NEW. Give points to the classrooms based on the items donated – more points for the items that are most needed. Please do not include used items.

Items we put in all our stockings

  • FOR ALL AGES: Hats and Gloves, Socks, Toothbrushes, Toothpaste, Shampoo, Hair Brushes, Bath Soap
  • AGE 12 to SENIOR ADULTS: Deodorant
  • AGE 15 to SENIOR ADULTS: Razors (both genders) and Shaving Cream (males only)
  • NEW and age appropriate gifts and stuffed animals for tops of stockings

*Especially needed are small items for men and teen boys such as: wallets, key chains, movie and fast food gift cards, bus passes, card games, all in one tools, water bottles, watches, flashlights, alarm clocks or small radios.

For our Senior Adults, we can use large print word search books, large print playing cards, large print puzzles, nightlights and flashlights, gift cards for grocery stores, stationary & stamps. Also, appreciate are very mild soaps and moisturizing lotions. Small canned hams, can be given to our seniors.

For Newborns to Age 2, any and all baby items. Clothing starting at size 6 months (they grow too quickly). Please nothing with lead. Please only BPA Free Bottles and such, and no stuffed animals with hard eyes.
