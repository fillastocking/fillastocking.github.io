Donate items all year long!

Donate Items:


warm/hats/gloves/socks, toothpaste, toothbrushes, shampoo and conditioner (16 oz or less), bar soap, deodorant, brushes, combs (no points), face masks, chap stick, sanitizer hand lotions in small to medium containers.

Items for the Homeless:


Mylar blankets, small fleece blankets, rain ponchos, hand and foot warmers, thicker socks and gloves, small first aid kits.


Donated items can be dropped off year-round at Spring Creek Coffee House, 10600 SE McLoughlin Blvd, Milwaukie OR 97222


Monetary donations really help us purchase needed items not filled through regular donations. Funds are also used to buy items in bulk at lower prices.


Donate through PayPal on our website www.fillastocking.org


Visit our Amazon wish list and purchase items directly through our Amazon wish list. Items will ship to our treasurer's address.


If you go to AmazonSmile and choose Fill a Stocking Fill a Heart as the charity to donate when you make a purchase you will automatically make a donation us.


Link us to your Fred Meyer Rewards by going to www.fredmeyer.com/communityrewards using our non-profit# 91156. You still keep your rewards!


You can volunteer to crochet or knit hats and scarves, donate yarn, donate Christmas material for stockings, sew stockings, donate cotton material for pillowcases and sew pillowcases (especially for men).

Items we put in all our stockings

  • FOR ALL AGES: Hats and Gloves, Socks, Toothbrushes, Toothpaste, Shampoo, Hair Brushes, Bath Soap
  • AGE 12 to SENIOR ADULTS: Deodorant
  • AGE 15 to SENIOR ADULTS: Razors (both genders) and Shaving Cream (males only)
  • NEW and age appropriate gifts and stuffed animals for tops of stockings

*Especially needed are small items for men and teen boys such as: wallets, key chains, movie and fast food gift cards, bus passes, card games, all in one tools, water bottles, watches, flashlights, alarm clocks or small radios.

For our Senior Adults, we can use large print word search books, large print playing cards, large print puzzles, nightlights and flashlights, gift cards for grocery stores, stationary & stamps. Also, appreciate are very mild soaps and moisturizing lotions. Small canned hams, can be given to our seniors.

For Newborns to Age 2, any and all baby items. Clothing starting at size 6 months (they grow too quickly). Please nothing with lead. Please only BPA Free Bottles and such, and no stuffed animals with hard eyes.
