Our Drop Off Sites

We appreciate the support of many community businesses and organizations that allow us to use them as a Drop-Off Site.

By being a drop-off site, businesses agree to have a decorated box on-site beginning in October. In it are empty stockings and instructions for the public on how to fill a stocking. Filled stockings are returned to the sites and picked up by volunteers.

  • Please leave gifts unwrapped and only include new items.
  • Return the filled stocking by the First Friday in December to the business where you picked it up or to any of the locations listed below.
  • Many agencies request stockings in early December, so early November turn-ins are greatly appreciated!
